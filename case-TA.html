The Problem

Planning international trips are never easy, especially when there are barriers such as unknown cultural customs, not being able to communicate because of language differences, being unfamiliar with medical and visa requirements, and navigating fooreign environments.

Who are we trying to impact?

Our persona Sabrina, represents their audience. A 26 year old, college educated - go-getter with a passion for helping people. Helping her roommate get out of a recent domestic violence situation sparked her curiousity in how she might be able to have more of an impact locally and politically.

What was the business challenge?

WRCDV’s intention was to broaden their reach to millenials, and redesign their website to make it more engaging and user friendly.
