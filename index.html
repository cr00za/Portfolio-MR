My Process

Discover

Identify and explore the problem space with objectivity, empathy, and a sense of adventure.

Define

Narrow in on the design constraints/ guiderails. Identify what we're solving and for whom.

Develop

Acquisitively test ideas with clear metrics to garner key insights. Strategically iterate based on analysis.

Deliver

Create an awesome solution from validated methodology - making it testable and improvable.

About Me

My personal mantra:

Design conversations and solutions that appeal to the heart of people and keep them engaged in a way that compliments the brand.

Why UX/UI?

I began my career as a UX/UI designer after several years as an electrical engineering consultant. UX/UI gave me a way to build on my skills in project management, collaboration, and meticulousness with detail to design creative human-centric solutions.

What has influenced me?

I’ve lived in and traveled to 15 countries including Japan, Austria, and Australia. But no matter where I go I always make new friends and create a space in what began as unfamiliar.

In my spare time I enjoy silversmithing, the guitar, anime, sculpting, running marathons, working with a nonprofit - Heal Georgia, and learning the strategy game of Go.
